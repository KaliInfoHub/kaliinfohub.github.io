        Kali Linux
       is a powerful, Debian-based operating system built for cybersecurity professionals and ethical hackers. It comes pre-installed with hundreds of great tools for things like penetration testing, digital forensics, reverse engineering and so much more!

    
So whether you're scanning networks with 
      Nmap, capturing packets with 
      Wireshark, or simulating attacks with 
      Metasploit, Kali is the ultimate playground for learning and practicing cybersecurity and ethical hacking.

        Kali Linux
       is used by penetration testers to assess the security of networks, by forensic analysts to investigate cybercrime, and by educators to train the next generation of cybersecurity professionals.

    
Examples include ethical hacking competitions, security audits, malware analysis, and red team simulations.
